IQR Outlier Method: For each device's usage (MB), we compute Q1 (25th percentile), Q3 (75th percentile), and IQR = Q3 − Q1. Devices below Q1 − 1.5 × IQR are flagged as low outliers. Devices above Q3 + 1.5 × IQR are flagged as high outliers. Normal devices fall within the fences. The exclusion threshold removes low/zero-usage devices before IQR is computed.
